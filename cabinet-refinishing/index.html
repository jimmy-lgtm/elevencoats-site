Our Process

How We Get a Factory Finish

The difference between a great cabinet refinish and a brush-mark disaster is preparation and application technique. We spray every cabinet door and drawer front off-site using professional spray equipment — no brush marks, no roller texture.

1. Remove & Label

All doors and drawer fronts are removed, numbered, and transported for spray finishing.

2. Sand & Degrease

Every surface is sanded, cleaned, and degreased. Grease from cooking is the #1 cause of adhesion failure.

3. Prime

Bonding primer applied to all surfaces — boxes, doors, and drawer fronts. No skipping primer.

4. Spray Finish

Two topcoats applied with professional spray equipment. Doors hang and cure before reinstallation.

5. Reinstall

Doors and drawers are rehung and adjusted. New hardware installed if requested.

6. Final Inspection

We walk through every cabinet with you and touch up anything that doesn't meet standard before we leave.
