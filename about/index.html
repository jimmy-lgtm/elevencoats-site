Who We Are

Built in Phoenix

Eleven Coats Painting is a Phoenix-based painting contractor with over 25 years of experience serving homeowners, HOAs, property managers, and commercial clients across the Valley. We are licensed with the Arizona Registrar of Contractors (ROC #360099) and carry full general liability and workers' compensation insurance.

We don't run a franchise. We don't subcontract to the lowest bidder. Every crew that works under the Eleven Coats name is trained to our standard and accountable for their work. That's how we've maintained a 4.7-star rating across 131 reviews and backed every job with a 9-year warranty.

Our focus is the greater Phoenix metro — Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, North Phoenix, Gilbert, Chandler, Mesa, Tempe, Peoria, and the West Valley. We're not the biggest company in the Valley. We're one of the most consistent ones.
