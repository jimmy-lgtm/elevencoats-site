Serving Sun City

Your Local Sun City Painting Contractor

Eleven Coats Painting serves both original Sun City and Sun City West — the two foundational 55+ retirement communities in the Phoenix West Valley. ROC #360099, fully insured, and experienced with the specific approval processes and quiet-hour rules that 55+ communities require.

Sun City homes are mostly 1960s–1970s original construction with varying layers of paint and prior repair quality. Many of the oldest homes have alkyd undercoats that need bonding primer before a modern latex topcoat will adhere properly — a step most quick-turn painters skip, and a step we never do.

Sun City climate note. Sun City specific: decades of sun on the same stucco mean many homes have chalked paint surfaces that require a TSP wash or mineral primer before any topcoat will bond. We catch this at walkthrough — if your prior painter didn't, that's why your paint is already peeling.
