Serving Gilbert

Your Local Gilbert Painting Contractor

Eleven Coats Painting serves the full Town of Gilbert — Power Ranch, Seville, Val Vista Lakes, Morrison Ranch, Agritopia, Trilogy at Power Ranch, Circle G Ranches, Adora Trails, and the historic Heritage District downtown. ROC #360099, fully insured, residential and commercial.

Gilbert is one of the fastest-growing markets in the country, and we've painted in it since before Power Ranch existed. The mix is largely 1995–2015 stucco with occasional custom builds in Morrison Ranch and Circle G Ranches. HOA color compliance is strict in most Gilbert masters — we handle all the board submittals for you.

Gilbert climate note. Gilbert specific: irrigation overspray from mature landscaping causes hard-water staining on lower stucco walls, especially on north and east elevations. We treat these with a mineral cleaner and tie-in primer before finish coats so the new color actually sticks.
