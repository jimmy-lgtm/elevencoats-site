Our Process

Prep Is 60% of the Job

Most paint failures come from skipped prep — not bad paint. Every exterior job starts with a thorough prep phase before any finish coat is applied.

Step 1 — Pressure Wash

Every surface is washed to remove dust, dirt, chalking paint, and mildew. We wait for full dry time before proceeding.

Step 2 — Scrape & Sand

Loose, peeling, or failing paint is scraped and sanded to create a clean, bondable surface.

Step 3 — Repair & Fill

Stucco cracks, holes, and damaged areas are patched and textured to match the existing surface.

Step 4 — Caulk & Prime

All penetrations, joints, and trim transitions are caulked. Bare surfaces are primed before finish coats.

Step 5 — Mask & Protect

Glass, hardscape, plants, and all adjacent surfaces are masked and covered before any paint is applied.

Step 6 — Apply & Inspect

Two finish coats minimum. Final walkthrough with you before we close the job.
